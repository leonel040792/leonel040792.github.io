Too many people feel lost and alone in our city. We hope you will open up a connection with us so that we can help you know Jesus, be guided by his Truth, and filled with his Love.

GROUP AND PERSONAL BIBLE STUDY OPPORTUNITIES

BROOKLYN BIBLE STUDIES

Nearly every day or night of the week throughout the borough we host group discussions about Scripture. Come join the conversation or let us know when/where/what we should add to our current lineup.

Faith in Christ does not develop in isolation nor in a mob. We would love to connect with you personally (face-to-face or via phone, Skype, email, etc.) to help you find answers to your questions.

INDIVIDUAL BIBLE STUDY

CONFERENCE CALL BIBLE STUDY

Can't make it for in-person learning and fellowship right now? Join us for one of our weekly conference call group Bible studies! Message for details.

On Tuesdays, men and women enjoy focused, in-home gatherings to consider Scripture, pray together, and build relationships to strengthen one another to live as the Lord would have us to.

MEN'S AND WOMEN'S SMALL GROUPS

Do you want to understand what it means to have and live by faith in Christ? Join us at the Central Library on Saturdays as we study through the letters written to Christians found in the New Testament. (Sessions at 3 PM and 4:15 PM)
